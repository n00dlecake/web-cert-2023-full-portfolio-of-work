THINKIT

ART

/

An innovative art studio in central Dunedin for youth ages 7 to 15. where the collaboration of the design process and art making will enhance the imagination, Enrich unique ideas, develop problem solving skills and space to drive their own inspiration and also one on one support and guidance to grow and develop. Think it art is all about building creative community, a sense of belonging and supporting young artists to be the best they can be.

What to expect at Thinkit Art - students are introduced to creative projects and encouraged to explore different ways of interpreting them, we spend time developing ideas and thinking about how we problems solve while we create. Each student has the freedom to explore in their own unique way with support and guidance with problem-solving and learning new techniques. We offer a range of paints, drawing materials and collage materials as well as the opportunity to work digitally in procreate and try animation..
