Information

Tim Burton is a huge inspiration for me and many others around the world. He has produced many iconic movies including 'Beetlejuice','The Corpse Bride' and 'The Nightmare Before Christmas' to name a few. He has also created many gothic-styled drawings. The Museum of Modern Art (MoMa) displayed an exhibition of his work in 2008, which included 500 drawings, paintings, photographs, sketchbooks, moving-image works and sculptural installations.
