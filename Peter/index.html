About us

OTAGO SALT TADERS

"When you think about it, isn's food the real social network of the age? food is, and alway has been, that special bonding agent that connects family and friends... whether we gather for holiday celebrations, special occasions or the traditional family dinner, food, plan and simple, brings people together." Otago Salt Taders have a mission! to make great , hand crafted solar sea salt using local natural and organic ingredients to give you the most amazing culinary choices. Otago Salt Taders all natural solar sea salt is a labour of love. our salt is harvested from the pristine blue waters of New Zealand's ocean in marlborough on the South Islands east coast using the age old process of evaporation with sun and wind. We use a range of artisan processes to create rare, natural, exquisite products.
