Creating Independent learners - Thinkit Fie is a cognitive enrichment programme idea for individuals with a learning difference, Eg Dyslexia. Thinkit Fie's focus is on raising self-esteem, building relationships and enriching cognitive thinking and problem-solving skills.

Kiri Scott is Certified Feuerstein (Fie), practitioner who has developed a creative learning environment to support individuals find their learning doorways. Feuerstein is a cognitive method which is used to support and enrich cognitive thinking and flexibility with problem-solving skills. students work with pencil and paper exercises supported by a mediator to help them stay engaged while targeting weaker cognitive areas and learning strategies to help navigate these situations. To bring meaning and relevance to these new learnt skills and strategies, students practice using them through art making and cognitive games.
